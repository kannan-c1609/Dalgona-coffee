Dalgona Coffee Recipe

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Add Coffee powder to hot water
Add 2 tablespoons(tbsp) of coffee powder and 3 tbsp of sugar to 1 cup of hot water. The hot water must be boiling hot.
Step 2: Whisk, Whisk, Whisk until the coffee mixture turns fluffy
Using a hand held mixer, whisk the coffee mixture till the the mixture turns creamy. It would be light brown in color. Don't be lazy to whisk
Step 3: Serve it with Hot/cold milk
Serve the coffee cream with hot/cold milk. A straw and a spoon would be mandate. Now stir, stir, stir to enjoy this lockdown recipe!!!
